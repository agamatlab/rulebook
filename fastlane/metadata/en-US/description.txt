HABIT TRACKER AND ROUTINE BUILDER

Create personalized rules that define your ideal daily routine. Whether you want to build morning habits, establish evening routines, or track specific goals, RuleBook provides the structure you need to stay consistent and accountable.

DAILY HABIT TRACKING

Track your progress with intuitive daily check-ins. Mark habits as complete, monitor your compliance rate, and build streaks that motivate you to maintain consistency. The visual calendar view shows your progress at a glance.

GOAL TRACKING AND ANALYTICS

Monitor your personal development with detailed analytics. View weekly and monthly insights, identify patterns in your behavior, and understand which habits are working best for you. Track multiple goals simultaneously with category-based organization.

PRODUCTIVITY PLANNER FEATURES

Organize habits into custom categories like Health, Work, Relationships, or Finance. Set flexible schedules for daily, weekly, or custom frequency tracking. Add notes and context to each rule for better clarity and motivation.

ROUTINE PLANNER CAPABILITIES

Design your perfect morning routine, evening routine, or any custom routine that fits your lifestyle. The app helps you establish consistent patterns and build a personal operating system for success.

SELF IMPROVEMENT TOOLS

Access smart insights powered by pattern detection. Discover your most productive days, identify areas for improvement, and receive personalized recommendations based on your tracking history.

LIFE ORGANIZER AND PLANNER

Keep all your personal rules and habits organized in one place. Archive completed goals, review past performance, and continuously refine your approach to personal growth.

PRIVACY-FOCUSED DESIGN
