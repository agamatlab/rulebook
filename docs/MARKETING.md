## 💡 Key Value Propositions

### 1. **Writes Better Rules**
AI analyzes your rule text in real-time and suggests improvements. No more vague goals like "exercise more" - get specific, achievable rules from day one.

### 2. **Discovers Patterns**
After 7 days, the app automatically finds insights you'd never see manually:
- Which days you struggle most
- Which rules support each other
- Your optimal times for success

### 3. **Evolves With You**
Rules aren't static. The app suggests when to level up mastered rules, make struggling rules easier, or pause rules that aren't working.

### 4. **Catches You in the Moment**
Context-aware reminders detect when you're about to break a rule (like opening a shopping app when you have a spending rule) and gently remind you.

### 5. **Complete Privacy**
All intelligence runs on your device using iOS 17+ Natural Language framework. Your data never leaves your phone. No cloud processing, no data collection.

### 6. **Zero AI Costs**
Unlike other "AI-powered" apps, we don't pass API costs to you. All intelligence is built into the app using Apple's native frameworks.
